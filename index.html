Feb 23-24, 2018 @ Harvard GSD

InfuencexDesign Conference at Harvard

To design is to influence

  • .... the experience of individuals,
  • .... the fabric of communities,
  • .... the structure of organizations,
  • .... the dynamics of systems.

At the sixth-annual HarvardxDesign conference, we will convene today’s leading thinkers and doers to explore design’s influence across these four scales.
